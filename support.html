Subscriptions and Purchases

To manage or cancel a subscription, open Settings on your Apple device, tap your Apple ID, then tap Subscriptions.

If you already purchased Peptendium Pro but access is not showing, open Peptendium, go to Settings, and choose Restore Purchases.

App Help

For help with app access, purchases, restoring Pro features, privacy questions, or technical issues, email us and include the device model, iOS version, and a brief description of the problem.
